About Our Church

The Seventh-day Adventist Church of LaFollette is a warm and welcoming community of believers committed to loving God, serving others, and sharing the gospel of Jesus Christ. Join us for Sabbath worship, Bible study, fellowship, and community outreach.

Our Mission

To proclaim the everlasting gospel of Revelation 14 and to prepare a people to meet their Lord through Bible-based preaching, Christ-centered living, and compassionate service.
